How To Play

This is mastermind. If you are player 1, you have to insert your secret code. This is done by clicking on the colours. A colour can be used only once! If you are player 2, you have to make educated guesses on the code of your opponent. Each time player 2 guesses by clicking on 4 colours and then the checkmark button, player 1 reacts with how close you were; a white pin means you got a colour correct but not in the right spot, a black pin means that one pin has the right colour and is also in the right spot. You do not know which black or white pin corresponds to the guesses made. Player 2 gets 15 tries; best of luck!
